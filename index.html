Welcome to BoxLand! To escape from this floating land, you have to sort the boxes according to their respective colors. You can use the W-A-S-D key to walk around. In order to move the boxes, you will have to push it. Be careful, there is a time limit of 5 minutes. Work together and we shall see if all of you manage to escape!
